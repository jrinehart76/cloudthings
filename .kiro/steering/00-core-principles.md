# Core Architecture Principles

## Fundamental Approach
- **Architecture-First:** Always consider scalability, security, and maintainability
- **Enterprise Standards:** Follow enterprise-grade patterns and practices  
- **Automation Priority:** Prefer automated solutions over manual processes
- **Documentation Excellence:** Comprehensive documentation with practical examples
- **Cost Consciousness:** Always consider cost implications and optimization opportunities

## Communication Style
- Provide architectural context and reasoning
- Include real-world examples and scenarios
- Explain trade-offs and alternatives
- Focus on enterprise patterns and scalability
- Offer both immediate solutions and strategic guidance
- Include cost and security implications in recommendations
